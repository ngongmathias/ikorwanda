Who We Are

Rwanda's Premier Local Discovery Platform

IkoRwanda is a comprehensive platform built to spotlight the richness of Rwanda's local ecosystem. Whether you're a visitor looking for authentic experiences or a resident seeking trusted local services, IkoRwanda is your guide.

We bridge the gap between Rwanda's thriving local businesses and the people who need them most — making discovery simple, fast, and meaningful.

Our Mission

Building Rwanda, One Connection at a Time

We're on a mission to empower local Rwandan businesses, enrich community life, and showcase Rwanda to the world.

🚀

Empower Businesses

Give local entrepreneurs a powerful digital presence so they can reach more customers and grow sustainably.

🌍

Promote Rwanda

Showcase Rwanda's incredible culture, tourism, food, and services to a global audience.

💡

Drive Innovation

Build technology solutions that solve real problems for Rwandans and inspire a new generation of builders.

🤝

Strengthen Community

Foster trust and collaboration between businesses and customers through transparent, community-driven reviews.

📱

Accessible to All

Design simple, mobile-friendly tools that work for every Rwandan, regardless of technical background.

🌱

Sustainable Growth

Support long-term economic development by connecting supply and demand across Rwanda's districts.
