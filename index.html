Our Story

Built From Observation. Designed for Africa.

IkoRwanda was founded by Mathias Ngong after years spent at the intersection of advanced software engineering environments, academic ecosystems, and African market realities. The pattern was impossible to ignore: brilliant people with real skills, unable to find work that matched them. Companies wanting to go digital, unable to find partners they could trust. Technology arriving in Africa faster than the systems needed to absorb it.

IkoRwanda is the answer to that pattern. Not a temporary fix — a systemic one. By integrating training, work deployment, and enterprise service delivery into one continuous loop, IkoRwanda eliminates the friction between learning and earning, between talent and opportunity, between ambition and outcome.

We are registered and operating in Rwanda. We are building for Africa. And we are just getting started.
